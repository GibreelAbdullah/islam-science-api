NEW ADDITIONS

This section lists some of the most recent publications that have not yet been incorporated in the main sections.

 

• Dr. Shoaib Malik, Science as Divine Signs: Al-Sanūsī’s Framework of Legal (sharʿī), Nomic (ʿādī), and Rational (ʿaqlī) Judgements

 

• Shaykh Shams Tameez and Dr. Shoaib Malik, Course on Hidāyat al-Ḥikmah. The course serves as an introduction to Classical Islamic Philosophy and includes sessions on Natural Philosophy.

 

• Blogging Theology Video with Dr. Shoaib Malik, Recent Developments in Islam and Science with Dr. Shoaib Ahmed Malik. Here Dr. Shoaib discusses the recent developments related to Islam & Science in mainstream academia, including university level course, PhD opportunities, and online conference.

 

• Dr. Shoaib Malik and Dr. Karim Koscenda, Understanding Miracles in Ash'arī Theology: A Systematic Presentation. Written for students and ṭullāb al ilm, this article offers a clear, back-to-basics introduction to Ashʿarī theology on miracles—covering core metaphysical principles, 7 miracle types, and 3 key conditions for prophetic miracles.

 

• Mufti Muntasir Zaman, On the conflict between Science and Hadith: a framework for navigating epistemological tensions. A paper on dealing with the conflict between Hadith and empirical data.
