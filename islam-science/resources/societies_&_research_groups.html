SOCIETIES & RESEARCH GROUPS

  • Initiative on Islam and Medicine (II&M)

The Initiative on Islam and Medicine (II&M) is a research center at the intersection of Islam and Biomedicine. II&M focuses on improving the health of American Muslims, and on developing an academic, multidisciplinary field of Islamic Bioethics. The Initiative conducts scholarship, provides educational opportunities, and hosts events that bring together medical and social scientists, Islamic studies experts, and traditional Islamic scholars for interdisciplinary dialogue and research. Dr. Aasim I. Padela is the President and Director of Initiative on Islam and Medicine. They hold workshops, conferences, online and offline courses related to Islam and Bioethics. They also have ongoing research projects, an upcoming book, and several publications.
